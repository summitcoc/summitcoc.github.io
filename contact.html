Directions

Summit Church of Christ is located in Ludington, Michigan. We're easily accessible and offer plenty of parking for visitors and members alike.

For specific directions to our building, please:

  • Contact us using the form above
  • Speak with us after services
  • Call during office hours

We're happy to provide detailed directions and can even arrange for someone to meet you if you're visiting for the first time.

What to Expect

If you're planning to visit us, here's what you can expect:

  • Friendly Welcome: Our members are genuinely excited to meet visitors and make you feel at home
  • Casual Dress: While some prefer to dress up, you're welcome to come as you are
  • Biblical Worship: Our services focus on Scripture reading, singing, prayer, and communion
  • A Cappella Singing: We sing without instrumental accompaniment, as practiced in the early church
  • Children Welcome: We have nursery care and children's programs available
  • Fellowship: Plan to stay after services for coffee and conversation

Please don't hesitate to ask questions - we're here to help and want you to feel comfortable!

Office Hours

While our church family is available to help throughout the week, our regular office hours are:

  • Monday - Friday: Please contact us for current office hours
  • Weekend: Available before and after worship services
  • Emergency: Our elders are available for urgent pastoral needs

For immediate needs or emergencies, please don't hesitate to contact one of our elders directly.
