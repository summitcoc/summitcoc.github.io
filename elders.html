About the Elders

Shepherds called to serve and guide our congregation

The Role of Elders

Our elders serve as spiritual shepherds of the congregation, following the biblical pattern established in the New Testament. They are mature Christian men who have been chosen to provide guidance, oversight, and care for the spiritual well-being of our church family.

"And when they had ordained them elders in every church, and had prayed with fasting, they commended them to the Lord, on whom they believed." - Acts 14:23

Biblical Qualifications

According to Scripture, elders must meet specific qualifications as outlined in 1 Timothy 3:1-7 and Titus 1:5-9:

  • Blameless in character and conduct
  • Faithful to their spouse (if married)
  • Temperate and self-controlled
  • Respectable and hospitable
  • Able to teach and defend the faith
  • Not given to drunkenness or violence
  • Gentle and not quarrelsome
  • Not a lover of money
  • Manages their own household well
  • Not a recent convert
  • Has a good reputation with outsiders

Responsibilities

Our elders are committed to:

  • Spiritual Oversight: Watching over the spiritual health and growth of the congregation
  • Teaching and Preaching: Ensuring sound doctrine is taught and upheld
  • Pastoral Care: Providing guidance, counseling, and support to members
  • Decision Making: Making decisions for the spiritual welfare of the church
  • Prayer: Praying for the congregation and its needs
  • Example Setting: Living as examples of Christian faith and conduct

How to Contact Our Elders

Our elders are always available to provide spiritual guidance, prayer, and support. If you would like to speak with one of our elders, please:

  • Approach them after services on Sunday
  • Call the church office and request to speak with an elder
  • Send a message through our contact form
  • Schedule a meeting for more private conversations

Please don't hesitate to reach out - our elders consider it a privilege to serve and support our church family.

Prayer for Our Leaders

We encourage all members to regularly pray for our elders as they carry out their important work. Scripture reminds us to pray for those in leadership and to support them in their service to God and His people.

"Remember your leaders, who spoke the word of God to you. Consider the outcome of their way of life and imitate their faith." - Hebrews 13:7
